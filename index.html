About Us

Welcome to Venere Group - Creating Timeless Spaces since 1970

Experience the epitome of luxury and artistry at Venere Group, tracing its origins back to a small woodworking workshop founded in Famagusta, Cyprus, in 1970. Evolving over the years, our family business now specializes in providing high-quality Italian solutions, showcased in our Paralimni showroom featuring renowned brands such as Copat Life, GD Dorigo, and Santa Lucia.

With a commitment to customer care, quality, and design, Venere Group has become your one-stop destination for luxury furniture, home accessories, and flexible solutions. From kitchen furniture to doors and interior design services, explore our diverse range of offerings. Join us in creating timeless spaces that elevate living, both locally in Cyprus and internationally.
